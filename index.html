26mm, f/4, 1/1250s, ISO 500

Icha Tea. It's a neat little bubble tea shop on Spadina. Their prices are a bit higher but the interior vibe perfectly reflects the premium. They even have a little space for you to drink your tea and order desserts in way in the back. Thankfully, I don't think all the money has been put into the design of the shop; I had their chrysanthemum pu er tea, and I was pleasantly reminded of the familiar taste of chrysanthemum with just the right compliment of bitterness. Their signature strawberry matcha is also popular among guests, but I never got to try it yet.
